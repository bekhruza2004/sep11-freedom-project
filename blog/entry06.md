# Entry 6
##### 6/6/26

## Content

### In-Class Presentation
One takeaway from giving my in-class presentation was that I need to show my learning process better. I explained how my game worked, but I could have shown more challenges I had. I also need to speak louder and make more eye contact with the audience.

### Expo Elevator Pitch
One takeaway from my Expo elevator pitch is that I learned how to explain quickly and clearly. I had 60 seconds and I had to focus on the important parts of my game. I presented to the judges and students who visited my project and it helped me practice presenting to different people rather than my classmates.

## EDP
I finished the last step of the Engineering Design Process which is Communicate the Results. I communicated my results through my in-class presentation and by presenting my project at the Expo.

## Skills
One skill I improved was communication because I had to explain my game to judges, classmates, and other visitors. I also improved my attention to detail because small mistakes in my code could cause features like movement, collisions, or the bomb timer to stop working. So I got more used to checking my code and testing my game to make sure everything worked correctly.
